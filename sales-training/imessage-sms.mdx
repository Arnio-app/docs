## <Icon icon="message-square" /> iMessage vs SMS

**Definition:** Understand how AI differentiates between iMessage and SMS conversations and the impact on engagement.

### Key Differences
| Feature            | iMessage           | SMS               |
|--------------------|--------------------|-------------------|
| Delivery Status    | Read receipts      | Limited           |
| Media Support      | High (images, etc) | Lower             |
| Personalization    | More human-feeling | More direct       |

### Strategy Tips
- Use shorter, more direct messages for SMS
- Leverage media and personalization more in iMessage
- Track response rates by channel for optimization
