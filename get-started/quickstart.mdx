> Here's how to get you up and running in just a few steps

This quickstart guide will walk you through the process of setting up your account to the Replyblue Dashboard.

<Info>
  **Prerequisites**: Before you begin, make sure to check your email for the invite and complete onboarding.
</Info>

## 🔐 Access Your Dashboard

Once you've completed the onboarding process, you should have received an email to login to the portal.

1. Visit [dashboard.replyblue.com](http://dashboard.replyblue.com)
2. Log in using your email or Google SSO.
3. Once inside, you'll land on your CRM dashboard

### **💬 Start a Conversation**

1. Select an existing contact or create a new one.
2. Click **"New Conversation"** to open a chat thread.
3. Start messaging leads directly via iMessage.

  The AI reply feature will automatically be toggle on, so if you don't want to use AI then disable the feature.
</Info>

### 📊 Track Usage Limits

1. **Daily Message Limits**
   - View usage in the top-right banner of your dashboard
2. **Monthly Contact Quota**
   - Navigate to Billing or Usage Analytics for a full breakdown
3. Make sure to include:
   - **📞  Phone number**
   - **🙍‍♂️ Contact name**

### 📲  **Check Your Phone Line**

- Go to **Settings → Phone Lines**
- See which phone number is currently assigned to your account
- [Contact us](mailto:hello@replybue.com) if you need to add or change your line
