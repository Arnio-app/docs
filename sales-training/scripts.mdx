## <Icon icon="file-text" /> Sales Scripts Library

**Definition:** Pre-approved messaging templates used by both AI and human reps across stages of the funnel.

### Categories
- **Cold Outreach:** Intro messages with context
- **Follow-ups:** Nudges after no response
- **Handoff:** Transitions to humans
- **Closing:** Confirming interest and next steps

### Example: Cold Intro
Hi [Name], saw you’re leading [Role] at [Company] — quick question about how you’re currently handling [problem]?

### Tips
- Keep tone conversational
- Test and iterate based on reply data
