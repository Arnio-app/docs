![CRM Pn](/images/CRM.png)

### How to access the portal?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Access the Portal" icon="unlock">
    - **Accessing the Portal**\
      Go to: [<u>https://dashboard.replyblue.com/auth/login</u>](https://dashboard.replyblue.com/auth/login)\
      Use your credentials to log in and view your CRM dashboard.
    - **Start a Conversation**\
      From the CRM dashboard, click on a contact or create a new one, then use the "New Conversation" button to launch a message thread.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
