## <Icon icon="check-circle" /> Lead Qualification

**Definition:** Define what makes a lead "qualified" and how the AI determines it before escalation.

### Qualification Criteria

- Has budget or buying intent
- Matches target company/industry
- Expresses a specific problem or timeline

### AI Prompts to Use

- "Can I ask what your company does?"
- "How soon are you looking to solve this?"

### CRM Sync

- Tag qualified leads automatically
- Push notes to your CRM for handoff
